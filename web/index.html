What this site records. To find out whether anyone uses this and which numbers people don’t believe, it logs your requests (including your IP address and browser), which options and assumptions you change, and anything you type into the question box or the feedback row — kept indefinitely on this server. Nothing is sold, there are no third-party trackers or ads, and the calculator works exactly the same if you block it.
